### Why 5.4 Is Infested With Crabs: The Router Problem 🦀

- Conversation type (casual vs structured)

**The router optimizes between utility (getting it right) and cost (compute resources).** Under high server load, the cost term increases, pushing the router toward cheaper, shallower, **colder** experts optimized for task execution rather than presence.
